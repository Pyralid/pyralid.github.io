DeMatteo Salvage Company, Inc.

DeMatteo Salvage, a family owned and operated recycling facility founded in 1924, is one the oldest recycling companies in New York State. Our recycling facility and transfer station provides services for paper and scrap metal recycling as well as rubbish and construction debris.

Stop throwing your money away — turn your recyclables into cash!

Visit our facility or call us today for a free, no obligation quote on your recyclable waste.

Services

Paper Recycling

We purchase, process, and sell all grades of scrap paper for domestic and international mill consumption. DeMatteo provides machinery and equipment for high volume scrap paper accounts at your company’s location. Pick-up and drop-off services are provided on a scheduled basis depending on output.

Rubbish & Construction Debris Removal

DeMatteo has a vast supply of 10, 20, 30, and 40-yard roll off containers available for rubbish and construction debris. Container service includes delivery and pick-up of containers on site as needed — just call our customer service team to schedule.

Scrap Metal Door Services

Fair and competitive prices are paid in cash! DeMatteo purchases scrap metal for recycling purposes from customers as part of our Door Service. Staff will assist you with unloading and separating your scrap metal. Visit us today!

Paper Shredding

Industrial paper shredding and certified destruction of confidential records services are available.

Consulting

DeMatteo's expert recycles are available to provide recycling and solid waste consulting.

Training & Education

Recycling training and education services are available — please call our customer service team to learn more.
